BOSSES OF ELDEN RING


Welcome! The aim of this site is to provide a variety of information regarding the main bosses you'll be facing throughout your time playing Elden Ring. The drop-down menu on the navigation bar will take you to a page describing any one of the fifteen main bosses. Along with this information, there's included a couple tips that might help you with anyone you might be struggling with. Alternatively, this website also maintains a ranking of each boss in regard to their difficulty; this information is generated by the input of users such as yourself. If you wish to add to this existing data, feel free to use the button below (or in the navigation bar) to visit our survey and provide your input.
